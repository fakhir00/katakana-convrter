Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Katakana Converter collects zero personal data.

Overview

Katakana Converter is designed with privacy as a core principle. The entire application runs in your web browser using client-side JavaScript. No data is ever sent to any server, and we do not collect, store, or process any personal information.

Data Collection

We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, address)
  • Text you type into the converter
  • Conversion results or outputs
  • Browser fingerprints or device identifiers
  • IP addresses or location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data

How the Converter Works

All text processing happens locally in your browser. When you type English text and click "Convert," the conversion is performed entirely by JavaScript running on your device. Your text is never transmitted over the internet.

Cookies

Katakana Converter does not use cookies, local storage, or any other client-side storage mechanisms to track users.

Third-Party Services

The only external resources loaded by this website are:

  • Google Fonts — We load the Inter and Noto Sans JP fonts from Google Fonts. Google may collect standard web request data (IP address, browser type) when serving font files. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Children's Privacy

Katakana Converter is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any data from children or any other users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please visit our Contact page or email us at contact@katakanaconverter.com.
