What Is a Katakana Converter? 🎌

A Katakana Converter is a tool that transforms text from various scripts—English, Kanji, Hiragana, Romaji, and more—into Katakana (カタカナ), one of Japan's three writing systems. Katakana is primarily used for foreign words, names, onomatopoeia, and emphasis in Japanese text. For a comprehensive overview, see Wikipedia's guide to the Japanese writing system.

Frequently Asked Questions ❓

Short answers about our Katakana conversion tools.

What is Katakana and why do I need to convert to it? 🎌

Katakana (カタカナ) is one of three Japanese writing systems. You need it for foreign names (Michael → マイケル), loanwords (coffee → コーヒー), emphasis, and technical terms in official Japanese documents and web forms.

How do I convert English to Katakana? ✍️

Simply enter your English text in the input field above and click "Convert to Katakana". Our tool uses sophisticated phonetic analysis to determine the most natural Japanese pronunciation for your text.

Is this Katakana converter free? 💰

Yes, completely free! No registration, no payment, and no usage limits. Our tools run entirely in your browser for maximum privacy and speed.

What's the difference between Katakana and Hiragana? 🆚

Katakana is angular and used for foreign words and emphasis. Hiragana is curved and used for native Japanese words and grammar. Both represent the same sounds phonetically.

Can I convert my name to Katakana? 👥

Yes! It's one of our most popular uses. For English names like "Sarah" (サラ) or "Michael" (マイケル), our engine provides standard, accepted Japanese spellings.

How accurate is the Katakana conversion? ✅

Our converters achieve 95%+ accuracy for standard names and vocabulary using context-aware phonetic and morphological analysis engines.

What is full-width vs half-width Katakana? 📐

Full-width (全角) is the standard for modern Japanese text and web forms. Half-width (半角) is an older legacy format. Most Japanese forms require full-width characters.

How do I type Katakana on my keyboard? ⌨️

You can use our Romaji to Katakana tool to type sounds using Latin letters, or install a Japanese IME on your operating system.
